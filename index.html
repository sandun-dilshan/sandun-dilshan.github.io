Scholarship Statement of Purpose

Why I Am Applying for a Master's Scholarship

My journey in Information and Communication Technology has taught me that technology is most meaningful when it solves real-world problems. Growing up in Sri Lanka, I observed how limited infrastructure, security challenges, and transportation issues affect communities with fewer resources. These experiences shaped the way I approach every project, research paper, and innovation throughout my undergraduate years.

I now seek a Master's degree in Computer Engineering, Embedded Systems, or Artificial Intelligence at a world-class institution — not only to advance my skills but to develop technologies that create tangible societal impact. My work on the Smart Motorcycle Security System, combining biometric authentication, GPS tracking, and IoT-enabled mobile applications, has demonstrated how context-specific engineering solutions can improve safety, accessibility, and efficiency in everyday life.

I am committed to applying this knowledge in Sri Lanka — through industry, academic research, or mentorship — and to guiding the next generation of engineers who, like me, see a degree not as an endpoint but as the beginning of meaningful, real-world problem solving.
